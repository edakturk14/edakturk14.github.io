About Me

I'm currently a Developer Advocate at Hyperlane, where I focus on creating educational content around cross-chain messaging with Hyperlane and building a thriving developer community. My goal is to make cross-chain development more accessible and easier for developers to adopt.

In the past, I've worked across different industries, including companies like World Bank, GE Healthcare, and Cisco.
